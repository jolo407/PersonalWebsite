Bio

My name is Joel Otero. I was born in Bayamon, Puerto Rico, in 1986. I moved to United States when I was 3 years old. I am an Army Veteran. I was in the Army for only a year, i was seperated due to an injury. I have an associates degree in Computer Networking and have been working in the IT field for 9 years doing Desktop Support, Help Desk, and System Administration. I am married and have a 6 year old daughter. At the present moment I live in Plant City, FL.
